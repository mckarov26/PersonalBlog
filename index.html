My Travels

Maruia Falls

A stunning natural attraction located on the South Island of New Zealand, near the town of Murchison. This picturesque waterfall is renowned for its raw beauty and serene surroundings, nestled amidst lush native forests and pristine wilderness.

Washpen Falls

80 kilometres West of Christchurch, you will find Windwhistle, a small farming settlement. In the heart of a dense forest, surrounded by the lush greenery of towering trees. Amidst this natural paradise lies Washpen Falls, a majestic cascade of water tumbling gracefully over rugged cliffs.

Mt.Hutt

Located on the South Island of New Zealand, is a majestic peak that towers over the Canterbury Plains. Renowned for its breathtaking alpine vistas and world-class skiing opportunities, Mount Hutt offers a thrilling playground for outdoor enthusiasts year-round.

Hokitika Gorge

Nestled on the West Coast of New Zealand's South Island, is a natural wonder renowned for its striking turquoise waters and dramatic rock formations. As you approach the gorge, the vibrant hues of the water contrast vividly against the surrounding native bush, creating a scene of unparalleled beauty.
